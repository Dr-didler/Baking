Cobbler Recipe

Recipe:

  1. 6 tablespoons unsalted butter
  2. 1 cup flour
  3. 1 ½ teaspoons baking powder
  4. ½ teaspoon salt
  5. ¾ cup sugar
  6. 1 cup milk full or low fat
  7. 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  8. 12-15 ounces frozen fruit (Use your favorite mix of fruit. I use a mixed berry one)
  1. Preheat oven to 350F/176C degrees.
  2. Melt 6 tablespoons butter in 8x8 or 9x9 pan in microwave or can melt it directly in the pan while it’s preheating in the oven.
  3. a large bowl, sift together 1 cup flour, 1 ½ teaspoons baking powder, and ½ teaspoon salt. Add ¾ sugar, 1 cup milk, and 1 teaspoon vanilla to the flour mixture and mix well.
  4. Pour the batter over the melted butter in the pan. Add the 12-15 ounces of frozen fruit on top of the batter and DO NOT STIR. Bake for 45-50 minutes or until the cobbler is thoroughly cooked in middle and it’s golden brown.
  5. Serve with homemade whipped cream.
