Prep

  1. Preheat oven to 350 F
  2. Grease an 8.5 by 4.5 inch loaf pan
  3. Take out a mit to set the dish on after the oven

Recipe:

  1. 1 and 1/3 cup flour
  2. 3/4 teaspoon salt
  3. 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  4. 1/4 teaspoon baking powder
  5. mix the previous four ingredients
  6. then mix the following:
  7. 5 and 1/3 tbsp butter
  8. 2/3 cup sugar
  9. mix these in after everything els has been mixed.
  10. mix in two large eggs
  11. 2 cups mushed bannana
  12. mix in half a cup of chocolate chips
  13. thrown in oven for 55 min
